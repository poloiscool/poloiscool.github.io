Having graduated with a first in ba (hons) architecture from central saint martins, i am a passionate,
engaged and endlessly inquisitive architecture student, who works in an intuitive and creative multi-disciplinary manner,
always eager to find new and exciting work processes that lead to innovative, thought provoking design proposals...
my foundation diploma was essential to my creative education, and afforded me the advantage of spending time making mistakes,
inventing problems and exploring the boundaries of spatial design through experimental model making, digital creative interpretations
of space and a solid grounding in illustrative representation. over the summer post-foundation, i explored synthesiser programming,
tailoring, audio visual coding and sculpture; creating music, bespoke garments, digital coded art and metal-work sculpture, engaging
and winning an installation competition at the chelsea physic Garden in London. After this, i went on to ba Architecture, using my
accumulated knowledge and passion to investigate the boundaries of the course. i loved exploring the university, engaging both socially
and practically with the other creative disciplines. alongside second year studies, i worked collaboratively alongside other architecture
students to design and built the fashion bA final year exhibition showcase. in the summer of 2022, i joined another small team of
students on a trip to Logrono in Rioja, spain to compete with international design teams in the concentrico spatial design competition.
